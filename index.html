EcoMyth Buster

Tap on a myth to reveal the science-based fact behind it.

Myth: Climate change is just a natural cycle of the Earth.

Fact:

While natural cycles do influence climate, the current changes are happening at an unprecedented rate due to human activities like burning fossil fuels and deforestation.

Myth: Renewable energy is unreliable and can’t replace fossil fuels.

Fact:

Advances in technology have made renewable energy sources like solar and wind reliable, cost-effective, and capable of providing consistent power with proper energy storage systems.

Myth: The planet isn’t really warming; it’s just weather variability.

Fact:

Weather variability happens over days or weeks, while climate change refers to long-term trends. Data shows a clear increase in global temperatures over the past century.
